Boston Celtics

The Boston Celtics are one of the most successful franchises in NBA history, with 17 championships. Founded in 1946, they play their home games at TD Garden in Boston, Massachusetts. Their team colors are green and white.

History

The Celtics were founded in 1946 as one of the original eight teams in the NBA. They quickly became one of the league's most successful franchises, winning 17 championships in their history.
